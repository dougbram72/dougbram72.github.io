36.0 PANDEMIC INFLUENZA-LIKE ILLNESS/COVID-19 PROTOCOL

All Providers

  • Don PPE prior to patient contact (mask, non-sterile gloves, eye protection), N95 if available
  • Mask on patient (surgical), over nasal cannula if necessary
  • Limit number of initial providers within 6 feet of patient/bystanders
  • Staff must wear face shield for invasive procedures
  • Consider cardiac monitor, pulse oximetry
  • Monitor, transport
Key Considerations
  • Notify receiving facility as soon as possible of respiratory infection patient, “CODE CHARLIE”
  • Encourage patient compartment airflow/ventilation
  • Clean EMS vehicle after transport while wearing PPE, outside if possible
  • Clean reusable PPE, goggles, documentation materials, etc
