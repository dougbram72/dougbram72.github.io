31.0 UNIVERSAL PATIENT ASSESSMENT

All Providers

  • Assess: Circulation, Airway, Breathing
    • If NO pulse, initiate Cardiocerbral Resuscitation Protocol
    • If pulse but NOT breathing, initiate assisted ventilations per BLS guidelines
  • Complete Primary and Secondary Survey
  • Obtain Vital Signs and Glasgow Coma Score (GCS)
  • Treatment Plan
    • Proceed to appropriate protocol(s)
    • Contact OLMC for questions
